### How Pedal pro works

### three step to have a best bicycle

Step 1: Input Your Weight, Height, and Personal Information:
Begin by providing Pedal Pro with your weight, height, and other relevant personal information. This data helps us understand your body type and cycling preferences, ensuring a customized bicycle tailored to your needs.

Step 2: Customize a Bicycle Based on Your Personality:
Using advanced AI technology, Pedal Pro analyzes your inputted information and generates a personalized bicycle configuration. This includes frame geometry, component selection, and other specifications that match your personality, riding style, and desired performance.

Step 3: Delivery of Your Customized Bicycle:
Once your personalized bicycle design is finalized, Pedal Pro initiates the manufacturing process. Our skilled technicians use state-of-the-art equipment and premium materials to bring your dream bicycle to life. After rigorous quality checks, we carefully package and deliver your custom-built bicycle to your preferred address. You can also choose the delivery time that suits you best, ensuring a convenient and hassle-free experience.
