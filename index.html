how it's works

Pedal Pro: Elevate Your Well-being in 3 Easy Pedals

01

Input Your Weight, Height, and Personal

Begin by providing Pedal Pro with your weight, height, and other relevant personal information. This data helps us understand your body type and cycling preferences, ensuring a customized bicycle tailored to your needs.

02

Customize a Bicycle Based on Your Personality

Using advanced AI technology, Pedal Pro analyzes your inputted information and generates a personalized bicycle configuration. This includes frame geometry, component selection, and other specifications that match your personality, riding style.

03

Delivery of Your Customized Bicycle

Once your personalized bicycle design is finalized, Pedal Pro initiates the manufacturing process. Our skilled technicians use state-of-the-art equipment and premium materials to bring your dream bicycle to life. After rigorous quality checks, we carefully package and deliver your address.
